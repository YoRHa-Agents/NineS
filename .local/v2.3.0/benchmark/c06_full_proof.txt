§7. Caveats / scope notes
-------------------------
* `SelfEvalReport` does not carry a `weights` field on the runner
  side (weights are computed in the CLI).  The golden fixture pins a
  per-mode `weights` constant that the trim helper layers onto the
  projection — this matches the CLI's emit shape without modifying
  the runner.
* The `overall` value in each golden fixture is recomputed from the
  rounded-to-3dp normalized scores so MockEvaluator-driven
  reproductions match byte-for-byte.  Floating-point tolerance on
  `overall` and per-score normalized stays at <= 1e-6.
* `requires_context: ClassVar[bool] = False` was added to MockEvaluator
  to satisfy the C01 Phase 1 Protocol requirement (the runtime-checkable
  Protocol now demands this class attribute).
